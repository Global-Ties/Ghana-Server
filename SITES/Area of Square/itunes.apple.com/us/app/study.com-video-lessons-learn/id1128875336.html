Description

The Study.com app will help you learn any academic subject on the go. Our short, fun video lessons, taught by expert teachers, provide homework help for difficult topics from your classes with easy-to-understand examples and animations. Ace your next test with Study.com’s online courses and test prep.

Learn Anything:
* Explore over 35,000 educational video lessons from Study.com
* Build confidence for test day with SAT and ACT prep
* Achieve any academic goal – from finding homework help to taking online courses for college credit

Learn Anywhere:
* Download video lessons to your phone to watch offline
* Study 5-10 minute videos at home, at school, or on your commute
* Track progress on all your devices – your scores and progress sync between the app and Study.com

Learn at Your Pace:
* Focus on the material you struggle with using self-paced online courses
* Text transcripts help you review tough subjects like math and science
* Retake quizzes as many times as you like and study answer explanations

Browse thousands of courses in subjects like:
* Math: Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, and Trigonometry
* Science: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Nursing
* History: World History, US History, and Western Civ
* English: Grammar, Reading, Writing, and Literature
* Psychology, Business, Social Science, and more!

Currently, you must be a member to enjoy all the benefits of the Study.com app. With a membership, you gain access to our entire library of resources for students of all levels: K-12, high school, college, and beyond. Whether you are looking for SAT or ACT prep, homework help, or teacher resources, Study.com has the tools to help you reach your educational goals.
