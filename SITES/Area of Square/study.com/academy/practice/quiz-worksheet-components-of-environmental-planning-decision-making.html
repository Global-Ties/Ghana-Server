About This Quiz & Worksheet

About This Quiz & Worksheet

These assessments will help you test your knowledge of the components of environmental planning. The quiz will highlight important topics such as the characteristics of environmental planning as well as the order of the three components of environmental planning.

Quiz & Worksheet Goals

Use this printable worksheet and online quiz to review:

  • Characteristics of environmental planning
  • The three components to environmental planning
  • The process of studying how humans make choices that impact the environment
  • Meaning of industrial symbiosis
  • Results of environmental planning

Skills Practiced

These simple quiz questions will help you practice the following skills:

  • Defining key concepts - ensure that you can accurately define main phrases and terms, such as industrial symbiosis
  • Interpreting information - verify you can read information regarding the results of environmental planning and interpret it correctly
  • Knowledge application - use your knowledge to answer questions about the three components to environmental planning

Additional Learning

To learn more about how to best protect our number one resource, review the corresponding lesson titled Environmental Planning & Decision Making: Definition & Components. This lesson will help you:

  • Define what environmental planning is
  • Understand the importance of environmental planning
  • Identify the three components to environmental planning
  • Describe how to make the best choices for the environment
  • Appreciate what sharing services and energy can do to help the environment
