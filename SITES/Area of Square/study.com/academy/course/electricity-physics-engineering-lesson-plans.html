About This Course

This course of lesson plans on electricity, physics and engineering can assist you in your teacher planning time. Work up homework assignments, discussion questions, quizzes and more from this resource, covering subjects such as the physics of flights, waves and electromagnetics. Our course is mobile friendly, so you can work on any device with an internet connection at any time you're able to. Use as many of the lessons and chapters as you'd like to help you with any of your curriculum needs.

How It Works

Check out this lesson plans, resources and activities grouped by subject into chapters for convenience.

Here you'll find:

  • Lesson plans and activities about engineering, physics and electricity
  • Videos you can show in class to engage your students
  • Lesson transcripts you can print out for classroom use
  • Games, activities and other resource to assist you

How It Helps

  • Engaging materials: The sample lesson plans and accompanying video lessons make it easy to teach your students about waves, electromagnets, and other physics subjects.
  • Offers discussion questions: You can use this chapter to start lively discussions that help your students understand electricity and physics concepts.
  • Stresses vocabulary words: These lessons cover the important physics terms and facts that are essential to comprehend electricity and engineering.

Topics Covered

Our lesson plans in this course cover subjects such as:

  • Machines in physics
  • Engineering
  • The physics of flights and roller coasters
  • Electricity
  • Waves in physics
  • Electromagnetic and light waves
