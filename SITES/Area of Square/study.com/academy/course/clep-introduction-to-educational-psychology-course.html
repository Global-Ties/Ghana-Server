Our video lessons make these topics engaging and approachable; they also provide you with an affordable option for earning a form of college credit accepted at nearly 3,000 colleges and universities nationwide.

Syllabus & Course Information

Lessons in this course are aligned with concepts covered on the Introduction to Educational Psychology CLEP exam. Use them to learn how to do the following:

  • Discuss the cognitive and behavioral perspectives in psychology
  • Outline developmental psychology in adolescents and children
  • Detail the role of motivation in learning
  • Explain individual differences in children
  • Identify different assessments of learning
  • Define instructional pedagogy
  • Describe research design and analysis

Prerequisites

This educational psychology course covers factors influencing children's cognitive development and behavior. If you've never studied psychology before, check out our introductory psychology course to get a feel for some of the key concepts and terminology used by professionals working in this field.
