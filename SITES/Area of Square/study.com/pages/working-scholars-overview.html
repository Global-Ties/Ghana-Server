How it Works

  • Earning the degree

    Students take most of their course work (up to 75%) on Study.com and then transfer to a graduating university. We have partnered with universities to ensure the lowest overall cost and ease of transferring.

  • Coaching every step of the way

    Our expert team of coaches proactively works with each individual student to help identify education goals, review transcripts of previous college credit and create a personalized degree plan.

  • How long it takes

    Pacing is flexible in order to fit any working adult's lifestyle
