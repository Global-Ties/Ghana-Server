Glossary of Career Education Programs

Many young adults have an idea of what type of career they would like, but they are unsure of the educational requirements needed to secure it. The Glossary of Career Education Programs includes information on the educational requirements for specific jobs and careers, including architects, biologists or a social worker.. Each article includes a detailed description of the career, required coursework and skills for obtaining that job, as well as an economic forecast, career outlook and salary information.
