List of countries by pineapple production

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Countries by pineapple production in 2016

This is a list of countries by pineapple production in 2016, based on data from the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database.[1] The total world production for pineapples in 2016 was 25,809,038 metric tonnes. Dependent territories are shown in italics.
