    GYPC 2018 Resources

    Introduction

    We hope that you had fun at Global Young People’s Convocation 2018. The conversations don’t need to stop there. The Planning Team and the staff of Young People’s Ministries are putting the following resources available for download so you can relive your #GYPC2018 experience and download any of the documents below for your personal or ministry use.
