Walk to Emmaus:

40 Years & Still Growing Anniversary Cruise 2019

Join us as we celebrate 40 years of the Walk to Emmaus ministry by going on a Caribbean cruise. Each morning you will have the opportunity to share in worship together. When we are at sea, you will have the opportunity to hear speakers talk about their Emmaus experiences and how it affected their Christian lives. You will hear Emmaus Ministries staff talk about newer ministries like Discovery Weekend, Chrysalis, Journey to the Table, and Face to Face, and they will answer your questions. When we are in port, you will have an entire day to discover Cozumel, Mexico. We hope you can be part of this time together, celebrating 40 years of Walk to Emmaus.
