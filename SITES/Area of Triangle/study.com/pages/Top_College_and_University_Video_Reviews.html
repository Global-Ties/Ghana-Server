Current Top 10 Viewed University Video Reviews:

University of California at Los Angeles Video Review

The University of California at Los Angeles is located in Los Angeles, California. The most popular majors are psychology, political science, history, sociology and economics. UCLA offers bachelors, masters, professional and doctoral level degree programs.

University of Pennsylvania Video Review

Pennsylvania University is located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Some of the most popular undergraduate majors at the school include finance, economics, history, political science and nursing. Pennsylvania University offers bachelors, masters and doctoral level degree programs.

Princeton University in New Jersey Video Review

Princeton University is located in Princeton, New Jersey about 10 miles out of Trenton. The most popular undergraduate majors at Princeton include economics, history, political science, public policy and English.

Texas A&M University Video Review

Texas A&M University is located in College Station, Texas approximately 95 miles northwest of Houston. The most popular undergraduate majors are interdisciplinary studies, agriculture, biological and biomedical sciences, psychology and business. Texas A&M University offers bachelors, masters and doctoral level degree programs.

University of Michigan Video Review

The University of Michigan at Ann Arbor is located in Ann Arbor, Michigan approximately 40 miles west of Detroit. The most popular majors for undergrads are engineering, psychology, English and political science. The University of Michigan at Ann Arbor offers certificates in addition to bachelors, masters and doctoral level degree programs.

Stanford University in Palo Alto, CA Video Review

Stanford University is located in Palo Alto, California approximately 37 miles southeast of San Francisco. The most popular majors are social sciences, engineering, interdisciplinary studies, biology and psychology. Stanford University offers bachelors, masters, professional and doctoral level degree programs.

University of North Carolina Video Review

The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill is located 50 miles east of Greensboro in Chapel Hill, North Carolina. The most popular majors for undergrads are communications, social sciences, biology, business and psychology. The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill offers bachelors, masters and doctoral level degree programs.

University of Texas Video Review

The University of Texas is located in Austin, Texas. The most popular majors for undergrads are social sciences, communications, business, engineering and biological and biomedical sciences. The University of Texas offers bachelors, masters and doctorate degree programs.

Yale University Video Review

Yale University is located in New Haven, Connecticut approximately 35 miles south of Hartford. The most popular majors among undergrads are history, political science and government, economics, English and literature and psychology. Yale University offers bachelors, masters and doctoral level degree programs.

Florida State University Video Review

Florida State University is located in Tallahassee, Florida. The most popular majors for undergrads are criminal justice, English, finance, political science and psychology. Florida State University offers certificates in addition to associates, bachelors, masters and doctoral level degree programs.
