The Basics of Undergraduate Education

Undergraduate education is the post-secondary education pursued prior to graduate study. There are many different four-year universities and colleges that provide undergraduate education. While some of these academic institutions concentrate on undergraduate education only, other offer graduate education as well. The cost of an undergraduate education varies depending on the school that is chosen. Admission requirements for undergraduate study also vary depending upon the program selected. These requirements are set by the institution offering the undergraduate education. For specific questions about undergraduate study, prospective students should contact their school of choice.
