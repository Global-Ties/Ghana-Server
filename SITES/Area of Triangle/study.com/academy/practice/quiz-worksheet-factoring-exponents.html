About This Quiz & Worksheet

About This Quiz & Worksheet

See how much you know about factoring and exponents with this interactive quiz. You'll be assessed on key points like how to find the greatest common factor in a given expression and how to factor a mathematical expression.

Quiz & Worksheet Goals

Answer questions on these points of study:

  • How to divide by the greatest common factor
  • How to determine which is the lowest exponent in an expression
  • The subtraction of exponents

Skills Practiced

  • Problem solving - use acquired knowledge to solve factoring and exponent practice problems
  • Critical thinking - apply relevant concepts to examine information about exponents in a different light
  • Information recall - access the knowledge you've gained regarding how to find the greatest common factor

Additional Learning

Find out about exponents and more with the lesson called Factoring Expressions With Exponents. The following topics will be covered for your benefit:

  • The factor of an expression
  • Factoring an expression with a greatest common factor
  • How to calculate/factor exponents and variables
