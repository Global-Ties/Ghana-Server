About This Course

In this course, we've included resources and lesson plans you can use to teach economics to your students. Whether you're just beginning or want to re-vamp your current lesson plans, the professionally written resources in this course can help you. You'll find plans here that cover Adam Smith, economic production and growth and much more. This resource is useful for a variety of educational objectives, from creating lectures to crafting interesting discussion questions that will get your students interested in this material.

How It Works

Our course contains chapters made up of individual lessons grouped together by topics that you can work through at your own pace and convenience.

The resources found in this course include:

  • Lesson plans that outline economics and how to effectively teach it
  • Video lessons that make economics interesting to learn about
  • Lesson transcripts that work well as study guides or handouts
  • Resources for games and activities that help students understand

How It Helps

  • Common Core: Ensures your curriculum meets all education requirements and takes the stress out of your planning time
  • Vocabulary words: Focuses on key concepts, individuals and dates that are important for your students to grasp in order to understand this information
  • Group projects: Helps you create engaging prompts for projects that help bring these concepts to life for your students

Topics Covered

Here you will review lesson plans on topics such as:

  • Economics
  • Adam Smith and his role in economics
  • Business and economic production
  • Economic recession and growth
  • Economic systems
