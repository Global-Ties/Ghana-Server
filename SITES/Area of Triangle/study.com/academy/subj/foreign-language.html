Foreign Language Courses

Study.com has an engaging foreign language to help you master a new language on your own schedule! Our self-paced video lessons can help you study for exams, improve your language comprehension, and boost your foreign language grade. Choose a course below and get started!

How It Works

  • Choose a course: Our foreign language courses will teach you the vocabulary necessary to tell time, discuss politics and take part in social conversations.
  • Watch fun and engaging videos: Our expert language instructors incorporate graphics and animations to bring foreign language concepts to life.
  • Test yourself with practice quizzes and exams: You can gauge how well you've absorbed each lesson by taking the corresponding quiz. Each course also features whole chapter exams that offer a more comprehensive measure of your understanding.
  • Study on the go: Available on your laptop, tablet or cell phone, our lessons can be accessed at a time and place that's convenient for you. Download the Study.com Android mobile app to learn a foreign language anytime, anywhere - even offline! We also have a Study.com iOS mobile app to study for foreign languages.
  • Get help from our beginning foreign language instructors: Our expert instructors are here to answer your questions.

Who Our Courses Are For

  • Students looking for beginning foreign language help or practice
  • Students who want supplemental materials for a foreign language course
  • Teachers looking for resources to teach beginning foreign language courses

Why Our Courses Work

  • Self-paced learning: Our courses allow you to set a schedule that fits your busy lifestyle. You can watch one lesson a day, or view an entire chapter in one sitting. It's completely up to you!
  • Ability to watch lessons over and over: Learning a new language takes repetition. With a subscription, you'll be able to watch each video lesson as many times as needed until you've mastered the language.
  • Unlimited access: A subscription is your key to thousands of lessons in multiple foreign languages, English, math, science and other subjects. These courses can help you boost your grades, earn college credit or enhance your career.
