College Courses

Use Study.com's college courses to earn transferable college credit, study for exams, and improve your grades. Our self-paced, engaging video lessons in math, science, English, history, and more let you study on your own schedule. Choose a course below and get started.

How It Works

  • Choose a course: We offer freshmen level/introductory courses in business, computer science, English, history, math and social sciences, just to name a few. We also feature study guides and test prep for students seeking credit-by-exam, as well as several certificate programs that are ideal for professionals seeking to improve their resumes.
  • Watch fun and engaging videos: Our brief video lessons were developed by experts in their respective fields. These professionals have incorporated animations, graphics and real-world examples into their lessons to keep students actively engaged.
  • Test yourself with practice quizzes and exams: You'll always know where you stand thanks to the lesson-based quizzes and comprehensive chapter exams that accompany each of our courses, study guides and certificate programs.
  • Get help from our expert instructors: Our instructors are here to answer your questions.

Who Our Courses Are For

  • High school or college students who are prepping for the CLEP, DSST or another college credit-granting exam
  • College students who have fallen behind or want to get ahead
  • Professionals who want to enhance their resume or LinkedIn profile
  • Lifelong learners who want to complete college-level courses for their own edification

Why Our Courses Work

  • Learn at your own pace: Watch our lessons at a pace that fits your lifestyle and your learning needs. This might mean you complete one lesson each day, or watch a dozen lessons over the course of a weekend. Your study schedule is completely up to you!
  • Choose where you study: All you need is a stable internet connection and a laptop, tablet or cell phone, and you can learn from the comfort of home, or take our courses on the go.
  • Access thousands of online lessons: With just one subscription, you'll have access to college-level courses in dozens of subjects. Take advantage of these courses to improve your grades, earn real college credit or boost your resume!
