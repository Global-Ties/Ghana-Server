Common Core State Standards Courses

Our self-paced video courses on Common Core State Standards offer a convenient and effective way to integrate Common Core in your classroom. From language and literature to algebra and geometry, our lessons can help you supplement your lessons and make sure they meet Common Core standards.

How It Works

  • Choose a course: We have Common Core courses on just about any subject and at multiple grade levels, including 8th grade math functions, 9th and 10th grade writing standards, general high school algebra and much more.
  • Plan your lessons: Courses can be used as a convenient planning resources for your curricula, units and lessons.
  • Screen fun and engaging videos: Our courses include sets of video lessons taught by expert Common Core instructors. You can screen them in class to supplement your lessons and prompt class discussions.
  • Test your students with practice quizzes and exams: Each video lesson includes a short quiz that you can assign your students and gauge their understanding of the material. Courses also come with comprehensive chapter exams and final assessments.

Who Our Courses Are For

  • Teachers seeking new, exciting ways to teach Common Core subjects
  • Educators who want to make sure their lessons meet Common Core State Standards
  • Instructors who'd like to enhance their curricula with engaging video lessons, practice quizzes and other supplemental teaching resources

Why Our Courses Work

  • Integrate lessons designed for the real world: Our instructors make some of the most abstract, challenging Common Core topics easy and fun to learn. Our video lessons are filled with real-world examples to help your students quickly grasp just about any subject.
  • Get the all-access pass: If you're interested in teaching more than just Common Core State Standard subject, look no further! With just a single subscription, you get full access to our huge catalog of courses on everything from college-level math and AP test prep to the history of Rome, psychology and world religions.
  • Plan lessons and homework assignments: The course material can help you plan your lessons and homework assignments before starting a new unit or subject area.
  • Easily integrate multimedia in your classroom: Our courses can be accessed on any Internet-connected device, which enables you to use the material wherever you need to. Lessons and quizzes can be assigned to students and your course Dashboard helps you keep track of their progress.
